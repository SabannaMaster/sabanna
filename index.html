About Me

Hello! I'm Sabanna, originally from Gulbarga district (Wadi Junction), living in Bangalore for the past 20 years.

I work as a freelance web developer, educator, and also in an NBFC company. I'm a YouTuber too — my channel "Sabanna Master" focuses on sharing knowledge and guidance.

I love exploring interesting facts, doing deep research, and learning new things from the internet.

My passion is to guide students about the real purpose of life before completing their studies. I help students score high, earn while studying, and build real skills.

I also empower women to earn from home and provide skill development training for children who are eager to grow.
